Brownies

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2/3 cup cocoa
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 300Â°F and butter a regractarian mold.
  2. In a small pot over medium heat, melt butter. Once butter is melted mix in the cocoa and set aside.
  3. In a medium bowl mix in sugar, flour, baking powder, and vanilla. Add eggs and mix everything with a whisk.
  4. Add the butter, cocoa mixture to the medium bowl and pour into mold.
  5. Place in oven for 30 minutes.

p.s.This recipe makes the best brownies in the world. To make the brownies even better you can add 1/2 cup of chopped walnuts.
